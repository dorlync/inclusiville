Element(Quick key): CSS Selector(s)

Headings (h): h1, h2, h3, h4, h5, h6; may be easier to use class="heading" for all headings: .heading 
Graphics/Images(g): id's for all images/graphics/logos, or class="graphics" for all graphics: .graphics
Main Region(q): id for <main id="main" class="region">: #main
Regions(r): class="region" for <nav>, <main>, <footer>, .region
Form controls(f): input
Paragraph(p): p
Links (k): a 
Buttons(b): class="button" assigned to all buttons: .buttons
Lists (l): ul
List Items (i): li
